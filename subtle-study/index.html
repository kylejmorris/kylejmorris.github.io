Sunday Hackathon 1 – Subtle Study

Kyle Morris

2020/08/09

Categories: Blog Tags: Hacking

While studying mandarin I found it tedious reading study cards 30+ minutes/day.

I browse the internet for 4+ hours a day.

Why can’t I learn mandarin while I browse the internet?

Now you can!

Introducing Subtle Study!

  1. Install the plugin

  2. Each day, new words will slowly, seamlessly be translated into mandarin.

    Day 1: All english text. :(

    Day 2: The word Cat is replaced with mandarin

    Day 3: More words are now mandarin!

    Click the red words to see the english meaning.

  3. Learn a new language while you browse the internet!
